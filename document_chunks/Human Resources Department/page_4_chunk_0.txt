PAYROLL MANAGEMENT CENTER  :
Staff remuneration is the University's main source of expenditure, so financial monitoring of the payroll
is particularly important and requires specialized tools processing the data entered by the individual and
collective management center. The staff assigned to this pole exercises social management control, coordination of payroll operations
and is responsible for the HR information system management tools. Its contacts are the general
management, the DAF and the rectorate services responsible for budget management. SOCIAL WORKER, PREVENTIVE MEDICINE PHYSICIAN, RETIRED :
As an employer, the University of Évry has an important social responsibility which it fully assumes. A social worker supports staff in finding solutions to their problems of economic, social, medical,
psychological, etc. origin. She is also a disability advisor.
